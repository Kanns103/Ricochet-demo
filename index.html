Instructions

Click the balls to make them disappear and more will take their place

Click the top animation for song choices

Your first time playing is usually hard and it will seem like you can't click the balls, just keep practicing and you'll find it!

If you click the section above the canvas you can hide it and the text within that section will change colour based on how many balls there are.

  • Green - 25 balls or more
  • Orange - 45 balls or more
  • Red - 75 balls or more
